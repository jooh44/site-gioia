# Prompts de Mockups para Nano Banana — Gioia

Documento de trabalho para gerar e iterar mockups de aplicação da marca Gioia no Nano Banana.

## Objetivo

Melhorar os mockups do frame `229:92` no Figma:

https://www.figma.com/design/Q3Z4u0sRw6iNkLDwUJLQ75/Gioia?node-id=229-92&t=3OM12mtyaWpJGIGI-0

Direção principal:

- reduzir o excesso de vermelho e calor geral da cena
- usar o bordô apenas nos ativos principais da marca
- manter o restante da composição em brancos, off-whites, stones e neutros claros
- considerar que a logo será enviada por upload ao Nano Banana
- preservar um tom de sofisticação boutique, premium, claro e acolhedor

## Base da Marca

Paleta oficial Gioia:

- Bordô principal: `#5E0001`
- Dourado da logo: `#d1af66`
- Base clara: `background` stone muito leve
- Complementares: brancos, marfim, off-white, stone, cinzas suaves

Regra prática de equilíbrio de cor para os mockups:

- `15%` bordô
- `5%` dourado
- `80%` neutros claros

Tradução visual:

- o bordô deve aparecer quase exclusivamente no ativo hero
- o dourado deve aparecer apenas como foil, emboss, hot stamp ou detalhe fino
- mesa, papelaria secundária, fachada, entorno, props e fundos devem ficar claros

## Prompt Base

Use este prompt como base antes de cada variação:

```text
Use the uploaded Gioia logo exactly as provided, without redesigning or reinterpreting it. Create a premium brand application mockup for a refined Brazilian law firm with quiet luxury, emotional warmth, and institutional credibility. Color balance must be controlled: burgundy #5E0001 only on the main branded asset, aged gold #d1af66 only as foil, emboss, or small accent details, and all supporting elements in white, ivory, soft stone, pale beige, and light neutral grays. Keep the overall composition bright, elegant, airy, and restrained. Neutral daylight, soft shadows, realistic materials, premium editorial product photography, clean visual hierarchy, minimal styling, sophisticated but not cold. Avoid overly warm color grading, avoid red ambient light, avoid orange cast, avoid making the whole scene burgundy, avoid excessive gold, avoid luxury clichés, avoid black-heavy styling.
```

## Pacotão

Este bloco complementa os prompts acima com versões mais prontas para iteração rápida no Nano Banana.

### 1. Master Prompt Curto

Use quando quiser um comando rápido, sem muita montagem manual:

```text
Generate a premium legal brand mockup for Gioia using the uploaded logo exactly as provided. Use burgundy #5E0001 only on the main branded object, aged gold #d1af66 only in refined foil details, and keep the rest of the composition predominantly white, ivory, pale stone, soft beige, and light neutral grays. Bright editorial lighting, realistic materials, calm upscale atmosphere, clean composition, elegant but not cold, premium print and stationery photography. Avoid red color cast, orange warmth, dark heavy backgrounds, or scenes where everything becomes burgundy.
```

### 2. Pacote por Mockup do Frame

#### A. Placa na fachada

```text
Design a realistic storefront or office entrance signage mockup for Gioia. The sign itself should be the only dominant burgundy #5E0001 element, with the uploaded logo rendered in aged gold foil #d1af66. Keep the facade architecture, window glass, sidewalk, wall panels, and reflections light and neutral, using white stone, pale limestone, soft beige, or cool light gray. The scene should feel expensive, calm, and clean, with daylight and balanced contrast. Do not spread burgundy into the surroundings.
```

#### B. Cartão sobre superfície clara

```text
Design a refined stationery mockup for Gioia with one burgundy #5E0001 business card as the hero piece, featuring the uploaded logo in aged gold foil #d1af66. Add supporting cards or papers in ivory, warm white, and soft stone. Use a pale neutral tabletop and restrained shadows. The image should feel editorial, premium, minimal, and airy, not dense or overly warm. The burgundy must remain concentrated in the main card only.
```

#### C. Pasta com documento interno

```text
Design a luxury legal folder mockup for Gioia with one burgundy #5E0001 folder as the focal branded asset and the uploaded logo in aged gold foil #d1af66. Include secondary legal documents, sheets, or inserts in white and ivory, partially visible. Use a clean neutral environment with soft daylight and realistic paper textures. Preserve a premium law-firm mood with restraint, clarity, and high-end simplicity. Keep all non-brand elements light and complementary.
```

#### D. Caderno e itens de escritório

```text
Design a sophisticated desk stationery mockup for Gioia with one burgundy #5E0001 notebook or agenda as the primary branded item, finished with the uploaded logo in aged gold foil #d1af66. Pair it with white notes, ivory pages, pale desk materials, and a few understated neutral accessories. Keep the scene bright, balanced, realistic, and editorial. Avoid cinematic darkness, excessive props, and warm red contamination across the image.
```

### 3. Variações de Direção Criativa

Use estas versões quando o Nano Banana acertar o tema, mas errar o clima visual.

#### Variação Clean

```text
Push the composition toward a cleaner and brighter result. Increase white space, lighten all supporting surfaces, reduce props, reduce shadow density, and make the environment feel more airy and minimal. Keep burgundy only on the main branded object and preserve soft neutral contrast everywhere else.
```

#### Variação Editorial

```text
Push the composition toward premium editorial product photography. Use more intentional framing, refined material texture, subtle depth, and elegant object placement. Keep the palette restrained and bright, with burgundy isolated to the hero asset and neutral whites and stones dominating the full image.
```

#### Variação Realista Comercial

```text
Push the composition toward realistic commercial mockup photography. Reduce stylization, reduce dramatic mood, use neutral daylight, believable material behavior, practical object spacing, and a clear premium corporate presentation. Keep the image polished but grounded.
```

### 4. Prompt de Correção de Cor

Use quando a geração vier quente demais:

```text
Rebalance the image so that it feels lighter, cooler, and more neutral overall. Remove the red and orange cast from the environment. Keep burgundy only on the branded item, brighten the whites and stone tones, reduce warmth in the shadows, and make the composition feel visually breathable and premium.
```

### 5. Prompt de Correção de Excesso de Marca

Use quando o gerador começar a pintar tudo de bordô:

```text
Reduce brand color spread across the scene. The burgundy #5E0001 must appear only on the main hero asset, not on background objects, shadows, reflections, or surrounding props. Convert the rest of the composition to white, ivory, pale stone, soft beige, and subtle neutral gray.
```

### 6. Prompt de Refino de Material

Use quando o mockup estiver com cara plástica ou genérica:

```text
Refine the material realism: elegant matte paper, premium coated cardstock, subtle foil stamping, refined embossing, soft natural reflections, believable texture, and understated luxury. Avoid glossy plastic surfaces, fake metallic effects, and exaggerated premium cues.
```

### 7. Bloco de Uso Rápido

Modelo prático para copiar, colar e adaptar:

```text
Use the uploaded Gioia logo exactly as provided. Create a [type of mockup] for a premium Brazilian law firm. Keep burgundy #5E0001 only on the main branded asset, use aged gold #d1af66 only in fine details, and make the rest of the composition bright and neutral with white, ivory, pale stone, soft beige, and light gray. Style: realistic, premium, restrained, editorial, elegant but welcoming. Lighting: neutral daylight, soft shadows, clear materials. Avoid red cast, orange warmth, dark heavy scenes, excessive props, logo distortion, and full-scene burgundy dominance.
```

## Recomendações de Uso

- começar sempre pelo `Prompt Base` + um prompt específico do mockup
- se a imagem sair quente, aplicar `Prompt de Correção de Cor`
- se a marca “vazar” pela composição, aplicar `Prompt de Correção de Excesso de Marca`
- se o material sair genérico, aplicar `Prompt de Refino de Material`
- se quiser testar linguagens visuais, adicionar uma das `Variações de Direção Criativa`

## Critério de Aprovação

O mockup está no caminho certo quando:

- o ativo principal em bordô chama atenção sozinho
- o dourado aparece com sutileza e não como protagonista
- o restante da cena parece claro, sofisticado e respirado
- a imagem parece boutique premium, não “luxo quente” nem “corporativo frio”
- a marca continua fiel à Gioia e não parece reimaginada
